Product Description

This handwoven cotton saree is crafted by skilled artisans from our women's Self-Help Group in Tamil Nadu. Each saree takes approximately 3-4 days to complete on traditional handlooms.

The saree features intricate traditional designs that have been passed down through generations. We use only natural dyes derived from plants and minerals, making each piece eco-friendly and unique in its color variations.

Our Story

Bunkar Mahila SHG (Women Weavers Self-Help Group) was formed in 2018 by 15 women from Kanchipuram district in Tamil Nadu. Most of our members come from traditional weaving families who have been practicing this craft for generations.

Our group was formed to preserve the traditional handloom techniques of our region while providing sustainable livelihoods for women in our community. We specialize in cotton and silk sarees using traditional designs and natural dyeing processes.

Through KrishiBandhu, we have been able to reach customers directly across India and receive fair prices for our work. This has helped us improve our families' living standards and send our children to school.
