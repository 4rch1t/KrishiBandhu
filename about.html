About KrishiBandhu

Connecting rural producers with markets through technology

Our Mission

Empowering Rural Communities Through Technology

Direct Market Access

Creating seamless connections between rural producers and nationwide buyers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices.

Digital Empowerment

Leveraging 5G technology to bring rural communities online and provide them with modern digital tools for business growth.

Community Support

Supporting Self-Help Groups and small-scale farmers with training, resources, and a platform to showcase their products.

Our Journey

From Vision to Reality

2020

The Idea Takes Shape

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we witnessed the struggles of rural producers in reaching markets. This inspired the vision for a digital platform that could bridge this gap.

2021

Successful Pilot Program

Launched our pilot with 10 SHGs in Tamil Nadu, focusing on handloom textiles and handicrafts. The results showed a 40% increase in producer income.

2022

Platform Launch

Officially launched KrishiBandhu, expanding to multiple states and introducing innovative features like live video product verification.

2023

Growing Impact

Supporting over 200 SHGs and 1,000+ farmers across India. Recognized with the National Rural Innovation Award.
